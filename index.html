How To Use

simple and functional

Prepare the Minipresso GR:

Start by unscrewing the water tank and filling it with hot water (not boiling) up to the indicated level. Next, take off the espresso cup from the bottom of the device and fill the filter basket with your favorite finely ground coffee. Make sure to tamp the coffee gently and evenly.big nice comfortable library, just for you so you can study well, welcome

Pump and Extract:Largest Play Ground:

Reattach the espresso cup to the Minipresso GR. Hold the device with one hand and use the other hand to pump the piston repeatedly. The pumping action builds pressure, forcing the hot water through the coffee grounds, resulting in a rich and flavorful espresso shot. Continue pumping until you see the desired amount of espresso in the cup.

Enjoy Your Espresso:

Once you've extracted the espresso, remove the espresso cup, and savor your freshly brewed coffee. The Minipresso GR's semi-automatic design ensures consistent and authentic espresso, allowing you to enjoy a delightful cup of coffee, whether you're at home, in the office, or on an adventurous journey.
